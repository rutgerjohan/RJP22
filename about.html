Let's talk me.

Hello! I am Rutger. After finishing my HAVO (Senior general secundairy education in The Netherlands) I started studying Communication and Multimedia Design at The Hague University of Applied Sciences.

Here I started learning about Visual Design (UI/UX) and the basics of Front end developement (HTML/CSS/JS). I soon found my passion for design and was very lucky to work with others that inspired me and helped me when I needed it the most.

I'm currently in my third year which means I'm still looking for an internship starting february 2023.

Some personal information

Sometimes it can be difficult for me to translates the ideas in my brain to words. That's why i like to use my creativity and art to convey a message!

And since you are reading this secret hidden message right now i guess that works!

Oh btw. My logo is an umbrella because in the Netherlands the weather can be very uncertain which means you always have to guess as to when to bring your umbrella.

How I work.

My workflow is usually based on Think, Make, Break, Repeat. Let me explain!



-1-
First, make sure you understand the problem. Speak with the people you design for and get to know what they need/want.

-2-
After understanding the problems you can start thinking about the solutions. Make sketches, Lofi's, Hifi's and start testing with the target audience. Repeat this untill you find a solution that fits best!
