  1. UX Design

    Foodsie

    For the project 'Foodsie' our goal was to design an app for kids in the age range of 8-13. The apps main function was to help these kids do the groceries on their own, as most of them will start doing this for the fist time around that age. This was definitely a challenge since we had to secure the safety of the kids as well as letting them only get the right items!
